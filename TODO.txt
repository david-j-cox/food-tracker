Food Tracker — Future Analytics & Features
==========================================

Dashboard Phase 2: Food-Symptom Correlations
---------------------------------------------
- Time-lagged correlation: fat intake vs symptom events (what did I eat
  in the 2 hours before each symptom?)
- Food tag vs symptom heatmap (which tags — fried, dairy, spicy, etc. —
  precede bad reactions most often?)
- Meal slot analysis: do symptoms cluster after certain meals?
- Cumulative daily fat load vs symptom likelihood

Dashboard Phase 3: Cross-Correlation with WHOOP & Strava
---------------------------------------------------------
- Nutrient intake vs next-day WHOOP recovery score
- Macro ratios vs WHOOP HRV and resting heart rate
- Pre-run/post-run nutrition vs Strava performance (pace, effort)
- Sleep quality (WHOOP) vs previous day's diet composition
- Iron/magnesium intake trends vs running performance over time

Other Analytics Ideas
---------------------
- Food variety score over time (are you eating the same things?)
- Meal timing heatmap (when do you eat during the day?)
- Nutrient gap analysis (which RDAs are you consistently missing?)
- Week-over-week macro trend comparison
- Recovery trajectory: symptom frequency/severity trend since surgery
